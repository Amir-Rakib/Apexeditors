BACKGROUND REMOVAL

Background removal or clipping path service is one of the popular services in the e-commerce and photography industries. It allows you to isolate an object from its background for further editing. Clipping paths or Background removal are often used in Photoshop to remove the background from an image.

IMAGE MASKING

Image Masking Service is a process to reveal some objects and hide some unwanted portions from a photo. The mask can be any color, but usually, it is black or white. When the mask is applied to an image, the areas that are masked will become transparent, while the rest of the image will remain visible. Apex Editors graphics designers have the authentic experience to make any kind of image masking.

FLATLAY EDIT

A flat lay is a great way of presenting your products if you can't or don't want to use a model or mannequin. Using various editing techniques, flat-lay images can be transformed into visually stunning works of art that leave a lasting impression on viewers. This kind of retouching is done to present a lot of variants of a single kind of product on e-Commerce websites. The retouching required in these kinds of images is usually background removal, liquefy, crease removal, and spot removal.
